üìù How to Add Your Photos & Video

To add your actual photos and video, replace the placeholder divs with <img> and <video> tags:

For images:
Replace <div class="placeholder-image"> with:
<img src="your-photo.jpg" alt="Description" style="width: 100%; height: 300px; object-fit: cover; border-radius: 15px;">

For video:
Replace <div class="placeholder-video"> with:
<video controls style="width: 100%; border-radius: 15px;"><source src="your-video.mp4" type="video/mp4"></video>

Upload your media files to the same directory as index.html in your GitHub repository!
